# üéπ Chord Progression Lab

An interactive web app for music producers to explore and create chord progressions with real-time audio playback.

![Chord Progression Lab](https://img.shields.io/badge/React-18-blue) ![Tone.js](https://img.shields.io/badge/Tone.js-Audio-green) ![TypeScript](https://img.shields.io/badge/TypeScript-5-blue)

## ‚ú® Features

- **Key & Scale Selection** - Choose from Major, Minor, Dorian, Mixolydian, Phrygian, Lydian, and Harmonic Minor scales
- **Visual Piano Keyboard** - Interactive keyboard showing available chords with Roman numeral notation
- **Progression Builder** - Build 8-chord progressions with click-to-add functionality
- **Real-time Playback** - Hear your progressions with Tone.js powered audio
- **Multiple Voicings** - Root position, inversions, open voicing, and drop 2
- **Sound Presets** - Piano, Electric Piano, Pad, Organ, and Synth Lead
- **Genre Presets** - Pop, Jazz, Blues, Lo-fi, R&B, Classical, and more
- **MIDI Export** - Download your progressions as MIDI files
- **Beautiful UI** - Dark theme with neon accents, pro music-app aesthetic

## üìñ How to Use

1. **Select a key and scale** from the dropdowns
2. **Click chords** on the piano keyboard to add them to your progression
3. **Adjust voicing** to change how chords are played
4. **Choose a sound** preset (Piano, Pad, etc.)
5. **Set the BPM** and hit **Play** to hear your progression
6. **Export to MIDI** to use in your DAW
